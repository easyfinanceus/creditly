Privacy Policy

This page explains how Kreditly.az handles data. The website is static, does not require user accounts, and does not maintain server-side personal profiles.

1. What data may be used

Some calculator values may be stored only in the visitor's browser through `localStorage` so forms can be restored on the next visit. These values are not sent to a backend server by this site.

2. Analytics and technical measurement

Analytics tools may be added later to understand site performance and user behavior. If that happens, this page should be updated with the specific services in use.

3. Advertising and third-party services

The site may later integrate advertising platforms, including Google AdSense. Those services may process device information, cookies, and ad performance signals according to their own policies.

4. Cookies

The current MVP does not include a dedicated cookie banner or advanced tracking setup. That may change if advertising or analytics scripts are introduced in later iterations.

5. Financial information disclaimer

All calculator outputs are informational only and do not replace an official lending offer or personal financial advice. Visitors should verify exact conditions with the relevant financial institution.

6. Policy updates

This policy may be updated as the website grows. Last updated: March 31, 2026.
